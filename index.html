From Prime Dental & Aesthetic Clinic

Prime Dental & Aesthetic Clinic, located in Mangalore, offers comprehensive dental care and aesthetic treatments tailored to your needs. With a team of highly skilled professionals, we provide services ranging from routine dental checkups to advanced treatments like implants, orthodontics, and facial aesthetics. Our clinic offers the best of consultants with over 6 specialists in varied fields like prosthodontics, implantology, endodontics (root canal specialists), pedodontists (child specialists), oral surgeons, and orthodontists to cater to your individual needs with utmost precision.

Comprehensive Dental & Aesthetic Solutions

Digital Outlook

3D visualisation of your teeth using state-of-the-art intraoral scanners and advanced radio imaging.

Hollywood Smile

Cosmetic smile design and makeovers using premium ceramic and composite veneers tailored to your facial proportions.

Microscopic Endodontics

In-house single visit root canal specialists using microscopic precision for hassle-free, accurate treatments.

Digital Implants

Digitally guided dental implant placements and complete denture treatments for perfect restoration.

Orthodontics

Preventive & Paediatric
